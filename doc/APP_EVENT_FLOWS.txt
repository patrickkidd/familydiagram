- No items on diagram

        * View.noItemsCTALabel.hide()
    - View.scene.itemRemoved

    - DocumentController.scene.timelineModel.rowsInserted|rowsRemoved|modelReset
        * DocumentController.onSceneEventsChanged()

        * DocumentView.hideBottomTimeline()
